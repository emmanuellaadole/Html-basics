12 Days of Christmas

Verse 1

On the first day of Christmas my true love sent to meta a patridge in a pear tree.

Verse 2

On the second day of Christmas my true love sent to me, two turtle doves, and a patridge in a pear tree.

Verse 3

On the third day of Christmas my true love sent to me three French hens two turtle doves and a patridge in a pear tree.
